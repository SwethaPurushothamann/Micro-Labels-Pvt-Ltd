About Us

Micro Labels Pvt Ltd is a premier manufacturing company based in Bangalore, India, specializing in the Screen Printing of Packing Cards for Precious Metals, Speedometer Dials, Decals, Overlays, Dash Board Appliques and Labels. With years of expertise, state-of-the-art machinery, and a skilled workforce, we pride ourselves on delivering precision and high-quality solutions to top clients across the world.

Located in the heart of Bangalore’s industrial area, our facility caters to a wide range of clients in the Gold Refiners & Minters and Automotive Sectors. We are committed to innovation, consistency, and meeting client-specific requirements with unparalleled service and professionalism.
